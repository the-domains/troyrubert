Harvard law school drops official shield over slavery links

Harvard law school will remove its official shield following months of protest at the symbol's ties to an 18th-century slaveholder. A committee at the prestigious university, which counts president Barack Obama among its alumni, was convened to rule on the issue and on Friday decided the shield was inappropriate.
